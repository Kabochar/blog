    第 16 天…

    1.What do you think?

    A: Do I look fat in this shirt?

    B: What do you think?

    A: I do?

    B: It’s way too tight. Your belly is hanging out!

    2.It doesn’t measure up. It doesn’t good enough.

    A: So, how’s the car?

    B: It doesn’t measure up.

    A: What? Your car is betterthan my Mercedes SLK320?

    B: What do you think?

    A: Mine’s a Volvo.

    3.(to be) watered-down

    A: What‘s the difference betweenDD and DDM？

    B: DD is the watered-downversion of DDM。

    A: So,DD is no good？

    B: NO！DD is great,bODMis MUCH better^^

    4.to jump the shark. something is no longer popular.

    A: Let’s watch the new Diehard movie.

    B: No way! Diehard has jumpedthe shark.

    A: What? Bruce Willis is the greatest.

    B: You’ re crazy!

    5.What’s your approach?

    A: What’s the best approachto learning English?

    B: I try to learn it like a child.

    A: What do you mean?

    B: I start with listening, repeating, and then tryingAA

    6.Bust a move! to hurry do something.

    A: Aren’t you getting married today?

    B: It’s 9:30.

    A: Already?

    B: You’d better bust a move.

    A: Thanks. I do NOT want to belate for my wedding.

    7.(to) hang your hat on (something). To believe something. To proud of something.

    A: Our team won yesterday! We’ regonna be the champions this year!

    B: You played who?

    A: We played against Duncan’s team.

    B: They’ re the worst team! You can’t hang your hat on a victory against Duncan’s team.

    8.busy work. do some work but no important.

    A: My employees have nothing to do.

    B: Give them busy work.

    A: Like what?

    B: Polishing thumb tacks! Cleaning keyboards!

    9.monkey business. something is illegal.

    A: Enough monkey business! Get back to work.

    B: Makeup is NOT monkey business.

    A: Is make up expensive?

    B: Yes.

    A: Will you buy more?

    B: Yes.

    A: Then get back to work! Earn some money.

    10.can’t help but do something.

    A: What are you doing?

    B: I’m eating more cake.

    A: You’ re supposed to be on a diet!

    B: But it’s SO good. I can’t help but eat it-～
