第 53 / 70 篇…

1. Heading VS going. Heading

A: Going somewhere?

B: I’m heading out.

A: Where are you going?

B: I’ll head over to Mike’s then home.

2. Will do. I will do it. You get it.

A: You going over to Mike’s?

B: Yeah,I’m on my way now.

A: Tell him I’ ll see him on Friday.

B: Will do.

Ps: Tell im I’ll see im(y~im) on Friday.

3. To put lipstick on a pig. 欺骗别人

A: What do you think of my business proposal?

B: Well,I don’t think it’ ll work.

A: What if I add more graphs and pictures?

B: That’s just putting lipstick on a pig. Sorry~~

4. What’s all this racket? 这一切为了什么？

A: What’s all this racket?

B: I’m trying to fix the sink.

A: It sounds like you’ re destroying it!

B: Then you do it.

5. Cut to the chase. 切入主题

A: I’ ve got a big favor to ask.

B: Go ahead.

A: Well, you know how I love you, dad. And I think you’ re the greatest, and..…

B: Cut to the chase.

A: Can I get 20 dollars?

6. Fingers crossed. Hope for the best. 祈求好运降临

A: John failed his exam.

B: Ha!I’m glad. He never studied.

A: But you told him you would keep your fingers crossed for him.

B: Yeah, but I crossed my fingers on my other hand when I said it!

7. Leave no stone unturned. 竭尽全力

A: Did you find them yet?

B: No.I’m still looking~

A: Leave no stone unturned. I need them.

B: How can anyone lose their glasses?

8. To travel halfway around the way to… 在半路上旅行

A: Oh~I’d travel halfway around the world to meet Celine Dion.

B: She lives about 10 miles from you.

A: Yeah, but she’d never meet me.

B: True, but I’m sure your heart will go on~~

9. Blah~~ So terrible.

A: You look a little..…

B: Blah~~

A: Yeah!

B: I am blah~Nothing’s going right in my life~～

10. ~ gives me a complex.

A: Let’s go to John’s barbecue this Sunday.

B: No. He gives me a complex.

A: What? Why?

B: His life is so perfect. My life is so blah~～
