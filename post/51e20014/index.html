    第 14 天…

    1.(to feel) boxed in.. 牢笼内

    A: Sue wants to break up with me.

    B: Why?You guys look happy.

    A: She says she feels boxed in.

    B: You are a bit intense-～

    2.(to) give a shout out to sb ~~ Say hello to sb.

    A: Shane, can you give a shoutout to me in your next lesson?

    B: Sure, if I remember.I get sobusy,I sometimes forget.

    A: I’ ll remind you every day!

    B: That might help!

    3.(to be) slick. It’s really cool!

    A: How can Bill Clinton still bepopular? He cheated on his wife.

    B: He’s slick. Nothing sticks to him.

    A: He’s like a snake, huh?

    B: Straight from the Garden of Eden!

    4.Straight from… direct from.. 径直的

    A: How do you like the new boss?

    B: Terrible!!

    A: Why?

    B: He’s straight from hell!

    5.(to) pay it forward. To pay it for the next person.

    A: What would you like today?

    B: A Caramel Macchiato. Large, please.

    A: That comes to $5.15.

    B: Okay… Here’s $10.30.I’ ll payfor the next person’s, too!

    A: Paying it forward! Great. Next＾＾

    6.a tar baby. The problem can’t ends.

    A: Do you think the West should haveinterfered with Saddam Hussein?

    B: It was a tar baby!

    A: How?

    B: Saddam is gone, but the West isSTILL over there. They created aproblem and it’s just gettingworse and worse.

    7.He’s 40 going on 20!

    A: Look at your brother!

    B: He’s having a great time!

    A: He’s 40 going on 10!

    B: Is it his first time to Disneyland?

    A: No! He comes every year! He’s a kid!

    8.You and me… me and you…You and I.

    A: You and me are just the same.

    B: You and l.

    A: Whatever~We both love English.

    B: And –you and me both hate to study And –you and I both like CoachShane’s lessons! And he likesyou and mel!!

    9.If I were in your shoes.. If I were you…

    A: Should I get the green shoesor the orange shoes?

    B: If I were in your shoes,I’ dget the purple shoes.

    A: Purple? That’s an ugly colofor shoes!

    B: So are green and orange!

    10.Keep them coming! Keep’en coming.

    A: Thanks for the question. Next?

    B: Come on, keep them coming.

    A: Why do you teach English for free?

    B: Because a lot of people don’t haveenough money to study.

    A: But you have to make money, too!

    B: People with money can join my DDM classes. Those classes are inexpensive, but they’ re not free. Next question!
