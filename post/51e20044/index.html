    第 44 天…

    1. Put your best foot forward. Do your best.

    A: My first day is tomorrow.

    B: Well, put your best foot forward.

    A: I’ll do my best.

    B: I’m sure you’ll do just fine!

    2. Slow jam.

    A: What’s your favorite slow jam?

    B: Oh, so many… Um, Let’s Stay Together.

    A: By AI Green?

    B: Yeah! It’s a classic.

    3. Before my time. Before when I was born.

    A: I heard you like Al Green!

    B: Yeah, so?

    A: Isn’t that a little before your time?

    B: It is, but his music is timeless.

    4. All the jazz. And so on. It seems like that.

    A: How’s DDM?

    B: It’s a lot of fun. Never boring.

    A: What do you learn?

    B: Pronunciation, how to speak, hot to listen..… all that jazz!

    5. Give me(gimme) some feedback. 给我一些反馈

    A: Would you have a look at my resume?

    B: Sure.

    A: I need some feedback.

    B: No problem.Send me an email.

    6. What’s your take? What’s your opinion?

    A: What’s your take on recycling?

    B: It’s essential.

    A: How’s America’s recycling?

    B: Where I live–it’s horrible!

    7. Clear your head. 清醒你的脑袋

    A: Where are you going?

    B: I’m too stressed out.I need to clear my head.

    A: Will you be back?

    B: After a while～

    8. Creep me out.

    A: Robots creep me out.

    B: love robots! Spiders creep me out～

    A: But you can kill a spider! A robot could kill you!!

    B: Hmm… Good point～

    9. Don’t jump the gun. 不要抢时间

    A: My garden is ruined.

    B: I told you not to jump the gun.

    A: But it was so warm last week.

    B: Yeah! And snow this week! May is the month to plant~

    10. In a heartbeat. 心跳加速

    A: I hate my job.

    B: Google’s hiring.

    A: Oh,I’d take a job therein a heartbeat.

    B: Yeah, but would they take you?
