    第 51 / 70 篇…

    1. Dead broke. 身无分文

    A: Did you hear what Hillary Clinton said?

    B: Now what?

    A: After Bill retired as president, they were dead broke.

    B: What? Dead broke! Tell her to come live with us! She’ll see dead broke.

    2. Bursting at the seams. 过于拥挤

    A: Did you like it?

    B: That was the best spaghetti ever!

    A: You want a bit more?

    B: No! I’m already bursting at the seams!!

    3. To bow out. To quit something.

    A: I heard your marathon is tomorrow!

    B: Yeah, but I had to bow out.

    A: What? What happened?

    B: My knee went out. I can’t run.

    4. To pass it on/along. 传递/分享它

    A: Hey, Brian’s birthday is Friday.

    B: Oh!

    A: We’ re gonna have a surprise birthday party for him. Pass it along.

    B: You bet!

    5. Mum’s the word. Don’t say anything.不要告诉别人

    A: We’ re having a suprise birthday part for Brian on Friday.

    B: Awesome!

    A: Mum’s the word, okay?

    B: Got it!

    6. My lips are sealed. You will not tell anyone. 我不会告诉任何人的

    A: Ryan’s birthday party is Friday. Don‘t tell anyone.

    B: You mean Brian?

    A: Oh, yeah. Brian. It’s a secret.

    B: My lips are sealed.

    7. A nail-biter. 一个钉子

    A: Did you see the Greece-lvory Coast match?

    B: Yes!

    A: That was a nail-biter!

    B: I’m loving these lesser-known teams.

    8. You name it. 不管什么

    A: You wanna name my cat?

    B: No! You name it^^

    A: What should I name it?

    B: You name it!

    A: How about… Shane?!

    9. To feign something. 假装某事

    A: Here comes the boss.

    B: He saw us watching the game.

    A: Quick–feign death.

    B: Were you men watching the game? Guys? Sue, bring my gun. Let’s make sure they’ re dead.

    10. Take a dip. To go swimming.

    A: It’s so hot～～

    B: Let’s take a dip.

    A: Great idea! The lake?

    B: Too far. Let’s go to the pool.
