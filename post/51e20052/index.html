第 52 / 70 篇…

1. To talk a mile a minute. 滔滔不绝的说

A: Did you understand anything he said?

B: No! He talks a mile a minute.

A: He should be a sports announcer!

B: Yeah! He’d be perfect for the World Cup!

2. A poor excuse. A lie. 蹩脚的借口

A: He broke up with you? Why?

B: He said, “You’ re too good for me.”

A: Oh ~ That’s a poor excuse! He has a woman!!

B: I know ~ He’s such a loser-～

3. Out of date. 过期，过时

A: What are you doing?

B: I’m updating the software. It’s out of date.

A: Oh,I should do that, too.

B: I’ll let you know if it’s any good.

4. A bug(bugs). A problem.

A: So, how’s the update?

B: There’s a major bug!

A: What happened?

B: My battery life was cut in half! I switched back to the old system.

5. A bug(RARELY plural). 细菌

A: Wash your hands with soap.

B: Alright～~

A: There’s a bug going around.

B: Okay!

6. … but no ~~~

A: Want some more vegetables?

B: No! I could have been having a barbecue, but no~~~

A: Quit complaining. The rain is nice.

B: I wanted hamburgers!!!

7. Cry me a river. Go ahead to cry but it’s not affect me.

A: Paris was wonderful but the wine was so expensive.

B: Uh-huh.

A: Really. We paid over 100 euros every night for dinner.

B: Cry me a river~~

8. Weather VERSUS climate. Short turn and long turn.

A: How’s the weather today?

B: It’s raining! It’s great!!

A: I thought it never rained there.

B: Rarely. The climate here is really dry.

9. What the…. What the heck? What the hell?

A: What the..…

B: What’s wrong?

A: The bank charged me a fee for putting money in my account!

B: What? Are you serious?

10. In a nutshell. 简而言之

A: Do you think I can use this song in my YouTube video?

B: Well, the copyright laws are very strict.

A: And complicated.

B: Yeah. But in a nutshell, if you didn’t make it, don’t use it.
