DailySpokenEnglish 171 180

2020年5月7日

第 18 天…

1. … my two cents. My opinion. My idea.

A: I think this color agrees with me.

B: Can I offer my two cents?

A: Sure!

B: I think the color is too girly.

A: Since when is pink agirlish color?

B: Since forever~~

2. Hit the sack. Go to bed.

A: Where’s Shane?

B: He hit the sack.

A: Already? It’s only 10.

B: He’s really tired.

A: He was up ' til one.

3. roly-poly. Nice Fat. Cute fat.

A: You’d better just have a slice.

B: But a slice might not be enough~You’ re gonna get roly-poly!

A: So, if I get roly-poly, you won’t love me?

B: Of course not!

A: That’s right. There will be more of meto love, so you’ ll have to love me more!

4. …in the shade. 在阴影下

A: Let’s move over there.

B: Under those trees?

A: Yeah, I prefer the shade.

B: Yeah, the sun is strong today～

5. made in the shade.

A: I heard John quit his job.

B: Yeah. His wife got a big promotion.

A: So, he quit?

B: All he does is golf now.

A: He should do house chores.

B: They hired a housekeeper! He’s got it made in the shade～

6. a shady guy. 偷摸的人

A: What did Dan want?

B: He wanted to know my phone number.

A: Did you give it to him?

B: No way! He’s a shady guy!

A: I know. Kinda scary~So, I gave him yourphone numberAA~~

7. a shadow. 影子

A: Shh! Someone’s outsideHow do you know?

B: I saw a shadow in the window.

A: Are you sure?

8. to shadow. To follow.

A: What are you doing?

B: I’m shadowing Sue.

A: Why?

B: John thinks she’ s cheating on him.

A: Cool! Can I come with you?

9. Beyond a shadow of a doubt. I am sure. I don’t doubt about it.

A: Who ate my cake? YOU?

B: No! Tim. Tim did it.

A: Are you sure it was Tim?

B: Beyond a shadow of a doubt.

A: How do you know?

B: Because his face wasall chocolaty!

10. The news knocked my socks off! The news was shocked me.

A: Did you hear that Nancyis pregnant?

B: I know! The news knockedmy socks off!

A: Yeah! She’s 62 years old!

B: She’s amazing! This is herninth kid!!
