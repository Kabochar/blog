DailySpokenEnglish 271 280

2020年5月7日

第 28 天…

1.A binding contract. Bound to contract.

A: I hate this internet company.

B: Switch.

A: I can’t.I signed a binding contract.

B: For how long?

A: I’m bound to it for3 years.

2.To bawl one’s eyes out. 眨了眨眼睛

A: What was wrong earlier?

B: Nothing.

A: You were bawling your eyes out.

B: I got a paper cut.

3.To be going through a rough patch. 度过艰难期

A: Hey, Shane.I haven’t seen you in a while.

B: I’m going through a rough patch.

A: At work?

B: No, at home. Lots of problems.

4.A slacker. 懒鬼

A: Tim makes me mad.

B: He doesn’t do much work, does he?

A: He’s SUCH a slacker!

B: Why does the boss keep him?

5.To slack off. To be lazy.

A: Playing a game?

B: Oh..… yeah~!

A: I’ve never seen you slacking off.

B: We all do sometimes!

A: I needed a break^^

6.What have you been up to? What have you doing seen last time I saw you?

A: Tim,long time no see!

B: Hey, Shane!

A: What have you been up to?

B: Not too much!Slacking off～

7.I’m stumped. I can’t work on the problem.

A: Did you solve Jazz’ s math problem?

B: No,I’m stumped.

A: I got it in two minutes!

B: Well..… you must be a genius.

8.Taken aback. To be shocked.

A: How did your mom take the news?

B: She was taken aback.

A: She’s not happy?

B: She’s okay, just very surprised.

9.Slack up. Be nicer.

A: You’ re too hard on your son.

B: He needs to learn.

A: You should slack up a bit.

B: No. Kids need to learn young.

10.To have a way with ~ To have connection with something.

A: Oh my god!A tiger!!

B: Come here, kitty-kitty~～Run for your lives!

A: Such a good kitty!

B: Wow–Shane really has away with animals.
