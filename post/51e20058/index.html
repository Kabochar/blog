Daily Spoken English 571~580

文章目录

第 50 / 70 篇…

1. Don’t quit your day job! Don qui cher day job！不要放弃你的日常工作

A: I started a new business.

B: Doing what?

A: I’m gonna sell handmade wool socks!

B: In the desert? Don’t quit your day job!

2. Sugarcoat. 粉饰，遮掩

A: So, how’d you like my poem?

B: Well… It was nice^^ Come on! Don’t sugarcoat it.

A: Tell me.

B: Don’t quit your day job!

3. Grab a nap. 小睡一会儿

A: Is there anything else you need?

B: No, nothing. Thanks.

A: Okay,I’m gonna go grab a nap.

B: Sleep well^^

4. Don’t quote me on that. 不要引用我的话

A: Coach Shane’s starting a new class next month, but don’t quote me on that.

B: Cool! What kind?

A: For speaking. Very necessary.

B: Excellent. I’m gonna sign up!

5. To be CHUMMY WITH ~ 和谁亲近

A: Why are you being all chummy with Lima?

B: You don’t know?

A: Don’t know what?

B: He’s one of the owners of Manchester United!!

6. To give someone THE BENEFIT OF THE DOUBT.

A: Where’s Mark? He’s late.

B: He said he got a flat tire.

A: And you believed him?

B: Let’s give him the benefit of the doubt~

7. Holy crap. 哇靠

A: Holy crap~~

B: What? What happened?

A: I just put vinegar in the cake mix.

A: You idiot! You fool!!

8. I lost my cool. 我失去我的冷静

A: Why is there spaghetti sauce on the wall?

B: Oh，..I，got a little angry last night.

A: And?

B: And I lost my cool. I’ll clean it up~

9. Have a go! 试试

A: You look like you need help.

B: Well，I’m trying to decide if I should try DDM.

A: Have a go!I think you’d like it!

B: But I’m too old~~

10. What gives? 怎么回事

A: I heard you told everyone I failed the test.

B: Well..…………

A: What gives? Huh?

B: I just didn’t want people to bother you~~
