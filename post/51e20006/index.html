    第六天…

    1. Stocking stuffer. 圣诞节小礼物

    A: Did you get any presents?

    B: Yes! I got a digital camera and some trainers. Oh，some lip balm as a stocking stuffer.

    A: You still have a Christmas stocking?

    B: Sure! Why not?!

    2. Keep me on my toes. 让我保持清醒

    keep - mi on my toes.

    keep-er on-er toes.

    A: Your spelling is wrong.

    B: Where?

    A: Here. You wrote “practice”，but it should be practiced.

    B: Oh! Thanks for keeping me on my toes ^^

    3. Keep it down. 小声点

    A: Come on! Keep it down.

    B: What! This song is SO cool ~~ It’s stupid. And the singer is HORRIBLE!

    A: That singer is my hero.

    B: And the song is so deep ~

    4.The apple of “one’s” eye! He is important for somebody. 某人眼中的小苹果

    A: Andrey really brightens up when he sees his girls.

    B: They’ re the apple of his eye.

    A: They look so cute!

    B: Fortunately, they resemble his mother!!

    5. Being picky. 挑剔的

    A: What can I get you?

    B: A hamburger.

    A: Anything else?

    B: Well，please cook it medium rare. Toast the buns and butter the bottom bun. I’d like twice as much ketchup as mustard. Three dill pickle slices. No cheese. A thin tomato and one strip of bacon. Am I being too picky?

    A: No，not at all，sir~~

    6. Turn out. 结果是

    A: How was the interview?

    B: It turns out they weren’ t hiring a clerk.

    A: Oh，no. That’s too bad.

    B: They were hiring a manager. And I got it!

    A: Wow! Everything turned out great!

    7. Auld lang syne. 一歌曲名

    A: Here.

    B: What’s this?

    A: The words to Auld Lang Syne. We’ ll be singing it at midnight.

    B: Oh! Thank you—

    8. Starting is half the battle. 开始是成功的一半

    A: I’m gonna leam English this year.

    B: Great. How?

    A: I’m not sure. Any ideas?

    B: YouTube! There are so many great teachers. Start with the one you like. Starting is half the battle, you know~

    9. Someone stick to his guns. Guns mean opinion, method, way. 坚持己见

    A: Doing YouTube videos every day is tough，isn’t it?

    B: It is. It takes a LOT of time.

    A: You should take a break.

    B: Not yet. I’m sticking to my guns. I told my students that I would produce a new video every day.

    A: I’m gonna do that ~

    10. Taking forever. Take a long time.

    A: You did yet?

    B: Almost.

    A: Come on! You’ retaking forever!

    B: Just a minute. Okay,～Come on in~Ugh!!!
