第 50 / 70 篇…

1. The thrift shop. 旧货店

A: New shirt?

B: Yep.

A: Thrift shop?

B: How’d you know?

A: Just a guess.

2. Kick off. To start.

A: When’s the season kick off?

B: Football?

A: Is there another season?

B: September 4th!

3. Pick it up. Go faster. Increase the speed.

A: Coach Shane speaks too slow.

B: You think so?

A: Yes. I wish he’d pick it up.

B: Leave him a message.

4. Gifted. 天才，多才多艺

A: What are you gifted at?

B: Sleeping!

A: No，serious! Tell me.

B: Hmm..… I guess I’m a very good explainer!

5. Mental. Stupid.

A: Where are you going?

B: I’m gonna go play golf.

A: Golf is so mental!!

B: No，it’s not. But，it is a very mental game~~

6. Short and sweet.

A: Oh, you’ re finished with the book?

B: Yep. You wanna read it?

A: Maybe. How was it?

B: Short and sweet. You’ll like it^^

7. In a pinch. 在紧要关头

A: Problem. We don’t have buttermilk.

B: You got. milk and lemon juice, right?

A: Yes.

B: Well，in a pinch, that’ll do.

8. Let it slide. To forget the problem.

A: I’m surprised to see you here.

B: Why?

A: You’re mom was so mad at you yesterday.

B: Oh，she always lets me slide. I’m her angel~~

9. For starters… 对于初学者来说

A: My English isn’t improving.

B: How are you studying?

A: Books. Sometimes movies.

B: Well，for starters，you should be watching E-cubed every day!

10. To patch things up. 修补

A: Come to the game with us!

B: You and Sue are back together?

A: Yeah. We patched things up.

B: That’s great. We’ll join you^^
