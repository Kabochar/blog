DailySpokenEnglish 341 350

2020年5月7日
| 阅读

第 35 天…

1.Starting is half the battle.

A: How can I master English?

B: Did you start DDM?

A: Ugh… Isn’t it too difficult?

B: Hey! Starting is half the battle! You’ ll be fine.

2.Kudos! 祝贺 paying respect.

A: Did you sign up for the free DDM lectures？

B: Yes,I finally did！

A: Kudos to you！

B: Thanks！Thanks for telling me about it^^

3.Snubbed. To be ignored.

A: And the Oscar goes to..…

B: Tom Hanks!

A: I can’t believe they snubbed you.

B: I knew I wouldn’t win.

A: But your acting in E-cubedis the greatest!

4.That figures. That make sense.

A: Shane got stuck on the mountain.

B: That figures.

A: Why?

B: If it snowed down here, it must have snowed a lot more up there.

5.Go figure. 去弄清楚

A: So, you don’t like carrots, but you like carrot cake?

B: Yep.

A: And you love tomato soup, but you hate raw tomatoes?

B: Yeah.

6.Nagging. 唠叨

A: Wash your hands before eating.

B: I will.

A: And clean under your fingernails.

B: I will.

A: And use soap.

B: I will! Stop nagging me!!

7.To blow off steam. 泄愤

A: And another thing–stop playing games all night.

B: Stop nagging me!

A: Where are you going?

B: I’m talking to you!

A: I gotta go blow off some steam~～

8.Bogged down. 越陷越深

A: Why didn’t you text me back?

B: I’m sorry,I was busy.

A: Didn’t you see it?

B: I did, but I was so bogged down at that time.

A: You don’t love me?

B: Ugh!!

9.A loose cannon. 我行我素的人

A: Shane’s videos are so stupid.

B: Don’t tell him that.

A: Why? What’s he gonna do?

B: He’ll punch you! He’s a loose cannon.

A: Ooh~I’m so scared!

10.He can hold his own.

A: Look at that guy.Still drinking.

B: And he’s fine!

A: He can hold his own.

B: How many beers is that?

A: That’s his 23rd can of beer.
