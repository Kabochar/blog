    第 54 / 70 篇…

    1. Peachy. 极好的

    A: Hi! Long time no see!

    B: Yeah，good to see you^^ How is everything?

    A: Everything’s peachy. Yourself?

    B: Couldn’t be better.

    2. To follow along. To listen careful.

    A: The first thing you need to do is call the US Embassy.

    B: That’s the first thing?

    A: Weren’t you following along? That’s what the travel office said.

    B: Oh，I guess I was just looking at the pictures on the wall~~

    3. sob，wail，whimper. 大哭，哀鸣，

    A: When I saw that puppy whimpering I wanted to cry.

    B: I was completely sobbing. It was so sad～

    A: I’m glad mom didn’t see it.

    B: I know. She would have been wailing~~

    4. Discipline (noun & verb). 学科 & 惩罚

    A: Ooh! Cake! Maybe I’ll get a small piece^^

    B: Aren’t you on a diet? You need some discipline.

    A: Who’s gonna discipline me?

    B: Me! Ha!I brought a mirror. Look at yourself!

    5. A longshot. 远景

    A: Do you think Shane will become a Hollywood movie star?

    B: It’s a longshot!

    A: Really? I think he’s SO talented.

    B: Yeah，but he’s bald! And old!

    6. Air quotes. 反话，双引

    A: Shane is SO “funny”.

    B: What? No he’s not.

    A: You weren’t watching. I was doing this..…

    B: Oh! Air quotes!!

    7. Hand-me-downs. 旧的衣服

    A: You’ re throwing away all those clothes?

    B: Yeah. They’ re hand-me-downs.

    A: Let me see them. Hmm…… I’ll take some of these.

    B: Help yourself. They were my uncle’s.

    8. A no-brainer. 一个明智的做法

    A: My English is good，but I can’t hear what native speakers say!

    B: Yes. That’s why I joined DDM. It was a no-brainer.

    A: Has it helped?

    B: Are you joking? ! I’ve improved so much!

    9. A dead giveaway. 一个破绽

    A: So, did you like the chocolate cake?

    B: Huh? Oh..… how did you know I ate some?

    A: Well, the chocolate all over your teeth was a dead giveaway.

    B: Ugh~~Sorry，mom^^

    10. To cut through the clutter. 脱颖而出

    A: What’s this?

    B: That’s my resume.

    A: It’s pink. A pink resume?

    B: Yes. I gotta cut through the clutter～~
