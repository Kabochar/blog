第 11 天…

1.To make ends meet.

A: I think I’ ll need a part-time job.

B: You already work!

A: But I can’t make ends meet.

B: Why don’t you try cuttingout things like cable TV?

2.Ordering take-out!

A: I’m ordering take-out!

B: From where?

A: Monkey Burgers.

B: Get me a cheeseburgerand a chocolate shake.

3.(to be) rather upset / angry.

A: I just found out I was adopted.

B: You must be shocked~

A: Well, I’m rather upset.

B: I would be, too.

4.(to be) perplexed.

A: You look perplexed.

B: Yeah.…. was that a manor a woman?

A: Oh, you mean Pat?

B: Yeah~I’m not sure, either, actually!

5.Keep it PG!

A: Don’t look over there.

B: Why? What’s going on?

A: Two people are kissing. GROSS!

B: Oh~Keep it PG!

6.A typo.

A: “Thanks you”? You made a typo.

B: Oh, thanks! Do you see any others?

A: Um… nope. Looks good.

B: Click-Sent! Thankyou for your help^^

7.Sooner or later. Eventually.

A: Do you see my lighter?

B: You haven’t quit smoking yet?

A: I will. Sooner or later.

B: Yeah. Sooner or laterit’ ll kill you.

8.Go figure~~

A: You’ve lived in Korea for over 20 years?

B: Yeah! Go figure～

A: Are you gonna stay forever?

B: I’m not sure!

9.Is A cosidered B?

Example: Is a tomato considered a fruit?

A: Is a McDonald’s Saladconsidered healthy?

B: It’s a salad!

A: But it’s from McDonald’s.

B: My guess is it’s healthy.

10.In my book… Means: In my opinion, …

A: Katy Perry is SUCHa good singer.

B: In my book, she’s not a singer AT ALL.

A: Then who’s a good singer?

B: Hmm… Freddie Mercury was a good singer.
