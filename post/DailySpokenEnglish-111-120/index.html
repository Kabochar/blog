DailySpokenEnglish 111 120

2020年5月7日
| 阅读

第 12 天…

1.Who are you! Einstein.

A: How can I fall in love?

B: Well, you need to stimulate your brain.

A: What?

B: You need an increase in dopamine and norepinephrine which will make you feel"love”.

A: Who are you? Einstein?

2.Beats me! I don’t know.

A: Is Pluto still consideredat planet?

B: Beats me.

A: Well, in my book it is.

B: Who are you? Carl Sagan?

3.(to) bite the dust! To dead!

A: Where is your friend?

B: What?

A: Your MP3 player!You’realways listening to it～

B: I dropped it,and itbit the dust~～

4.Bite me! Go to help! Don’t border me! Leave me alone!

A: Are you sick?

B: Bite me~

A: What? You look pale..…

B: Ugh!I just found out my girlfriendis seeing someone else!

5.No ifs, ands or buts!

A: I want that report onmy desk by 5.

B: 5? It’s 4 o’ clock now.

A: No ifs, ands or buts.5.

B: Alright…

6.I’m damned if I do, I’m damned if I don’t.

A: Are you going to thecompany dinner?

B: If I go, my wife will bemad. If I don’t go, theboss will be mad~

A: You’ re damned if you do, damned if you don’t!

B: Damn it..…

7.Hard to beat~~ 难以被击败

A: Who do you think willwin the match?

B: Between Spain and the US?

A: Yeah.

B: Spain will be hard to beat!

8.Out of the blue. Outta the blue.

A: Out of the clear blue sky. To express Suddenly.

B: You’ re all wet.

A: I know! Out of the blue itstarted to rain~

B: It rained?

A: Yes! It totally surprised　everyone～

9.Low-key~~ descript color! 不显眼的颜色。

A: My favorite actor is Tom Hanks.

B: Why’s that?

A: He’s great on-screen andlow-key off-screen.

B: You’ re right! He’s neverin the news like Lady Gaga!

10.What do you say we… How about do something..

A: I’m bored.

B: What do you say wewatch a movie?

A: Sure! What do youwanna see?

B: How about..… Titanic?
