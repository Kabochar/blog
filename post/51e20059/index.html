    第 59 / 70 篇…

    1. A rubbernecker. 好奇而东张西望的人

    A: Oh no! Be careful. An accident.

    B: Whoa! On the other side of the street, too!

    A: Don’t be a rubbernecker.

    B: Don’t worry. My eyes are on the road.

    2. Count your blessings. 往好处想

    A: Are you crying?

    B: No.I’m just so sad for the children.

    A: Yeah. War is so disgusting.

    B: I count my blessings every day.

    3. Close，but no cigar. 接近了，但还不是正确的

    A: How many states in the United States of America?

    B: 48

    A: Wrong.

    B: 54.

    A: Wrong.

    B: 51.

    A: Close, but no cigar. 50 is the right answer.

    4. Snazzy. 时髦

    A: Did you see Pavel’s team photo?

    B: The soccer team photo?

    A: Yeah. Pretty snazzy uniforms!

    B: But can they play?!!

    5. Chintzy. 小气的

    A: I’ lI give you 50 cents each time you take out the garbage.

    B: What?! Don’t be so chintzy，dad!

    A: Whoa! My dad gave me 50 cents，so I’m giving you 50 cents.

    B: Dad，that was like 50 years ago!!

    6. To move mountains. 终会成功

    A: Your daughter works so hard.

    B: I know. Her determination is amazing.

    A: She’ll move mountains one day^^

    B: She just might!

    7. To freeze my butt off. Super cold

    A: I heard you’ re moving to the mountains.

    B: That’s the plan.

    A: You’ re gonna freeze your butt off!

    B: Better than melting!!

    8. To ace something. 擅长某些东西

    A: So,how was the driver’s test?

    B: I aced it!

    A: Really?

    B: Well，I got 1 wrong out of 50^^

    9. Itching to do something. 渴望做点什么

    A: I’m itching to go to the mountains.

    B: What for?

    A: Just to be in nature. To be around trees.

    B: What are you? A bear?!!

    10. Pear-shaped. 梨形状的

    A: Um…I think you’ve put on some weight.

    B: I know.I’m totally pear-shaped.

    A: Your diet didn’t work?

    B: My diet went pear-shaped from day one.
