Daily Spoken English 621~621

2020年5月7日
| 英语
文章目录

第 62 / 70 篇…

1. Dinner and a movie. A simple day.

A: It’s been really nice talking to you. B: Me, too. Enjoy your stay in New York. A: I will^^ Um, if you have time, how about dinner and a movie? B: That sounds lovely^^ Um, let me ask my husband…

2. skip the middleman.

A: Nice cup. Where’d you get it? B: Amazon. A: Look，it’s made here in town! B: Really? Aw~I should’ ve skipped the middleman!

3. quarantine (noun & verb）隔离

A: What do you think about quarantining people? B: After visiting West Africa? I think its a must. A: But what about freedom? B: Freedom or public safety– take your pick!

4. to josh someone. 玩笑

A: Coach Shane received Halloween candy today! B: What? Are you joshing me? A: No. He went as a cowboy and got lots of candy~ B: But Halloween’s for children~ Oh my god!

5. earnest

A: What’s so funny? B: My English lesson. A: I thought you were a serious student. No time for fun. B: I am an earnest student. Serious does not have to equal boring!

6. What a joke~

A: What a joke～ B: What? A: President Obama’s Press Secretary’s name is Josh Earnest. B:Yeah，so? A: Josh-to lie. Earnest-very serious. So..… he’s a serious liar! B: Yeah~ What a joke!

7. Get the vote out!

A: Did you vote? B: No. A: Why not? Get the vote out! B: It doesn’t matter. A: Yes，it does! Polls are closing. GO!

8. a rain check

A: Wanna go to the baseball game tonight with me? B: I thought you went yesterday. A: It rained– they gave me a rain check. B: Oh~ Can I get a rain check，too? I have to work late.

9. use your noggin

A: How can I crack this walnut? B: Use your noggin. A: Oh，okay! OW!!! B: No~ Use your brains，silly~

10. to put a dent into something

A: I made this cake for my mom yesterday，but she didn’t even put a dent into it～ B: Awesome! Let’s dig in^^ A: Don’t you want a plate? B: No need!!
