第 17 天…

1.Keep it together. Calm down for relax.

A: Oh…I don’t know if I can.

B: Come on! Keep it together.

A: But I’m scared!

B: You baby! It’s only a90-meter bungee jump! GO!!

2.To hang out. To play. Let’s hang out.

A: Let’s hang out at the mall today.

B: Why? Let’s just hang out at home.

A: Home is boring.

B: The mall is the same! Staring at people and eating junk food. BORING!

A: Starbucks?

B: Okay.

3.To hammer something home. 将某东西深深钉在脑子里

A: You’ re still drinking diet soda?

B: Yeah. I’m fat.

A: What do I have to do to hammerit home? Aspartame is bad for you.

B: But I’m fat.

A: Aspartame comes from E. colipoop. It’s POOP!!

4.Sneak attack. 背后偷袭.

A: This cat video is SO funny!

B: Oh my god!Look at him fly!

A: Its brother made a sneak attack!

B: Brothers can be SO mean!

5.A sweet tooth. You like to eat sweet thing.

A: Someone has a sweet tooth.

B: Baklava! I LOVE this stuff!

A: Did you buy it?

B: No, it’s Anne’s. She’s got abigger sweet tooth than me!

6.All I/you gotta do is… 我所该做的是…

A: I wanna master English.

B: All you gotta do is study a little every day.

A: No.All I gotta do is findan American girlfriend.

B: Yeah. Good luck-～

7.Are you still smoking? - curious.

A: You are still smoking? - confirmation.

B: Are you still single?

A: You are still asking me?

B: Well, I do want some grand children.

A: Don’t worry, grandma! There’s plenty of time~～

8.Smooth sailing. 一帆风顺

A: I finished my report!

B: Already?

A: Yes! The rest of the weekwill be smooth sailing for me!

B: I’m so jealous~I’m noteven half done..…

9.To be on the rocks. Not healthy relationship! This time must be dangerous.

A: I haven’t seen Sue lately.

B: We’ re on the rocks.

A: What’s wrong?

B: I think we’ re just bored with each other～~

10.On the rocks. 用在酒上，意为加冰

A: Whisky on the rocks, please.

B: And for you?

A: Coke on the rocks!

B: We don’t say that. Wejust say"with ice”.

A: Oh..…
