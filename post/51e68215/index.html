    第 36 天…

    1.Back at you! 回赠，还给你

    A: 2 more days left in 2013!

    B: Yes. I’m glad!

    A: Happy New Year!

    B: Back at you^^

    2.Same to you!

    A: Have you made any resolutions?

    B: I’m gonna run a marathon in 2014. You?

    A: I’m gonna master English! Good luck to you^^

    B: Same to you^^

    3.Back to you!

    A: How is the celebration in Times Square?

    B: Still thousands of people!

    A: It’s so noisy!

    B: Yes! Too noisy!

    A: Back to you~Okay, we’ll have more later from Times Square.

    4.Standing in my way.

    A: You should focus on English.

    B: I know. But..…

    A: What’s standing in your way?

    B: I don’t have time~～

    5.More power to you.

    A: I’ve really reduced my smoking.

    B: How many today?

    A: 9.

    B: Great! More power to you!

    6.Industrious. 勤奋

    A: Wow! Did you clean your room?

    B: Yep. And I did the dishes.

    A: Great.

    B: And I folded the clothes.

    A: You are so industrious!

    7.To dwell on something. Be talking about one thing. 纠结某件事

    A: The boss is always dwelling on the importance of coming in early.

    B: Yeah. But he’s the firs tto fall asleep at his desk.

    A: He thinks he’s so industrious.

    B: I get more done in one day than he does all week.

    8.To give a holler. Call me.

    A: I heard you’ re moving next week.

    B: Yes!I’m excited.

    A: Well, give me a holler if you need any help.

    B: Thanks, Country Shane.

    A: I will^^

    9.Yikes! 哎呀

    A: Shane,I want you to give the opening speech.

    B: Oh, okay. How long should it be?

    A: 60 minutes.

    B: Yikes! Um, how many people will be there?

    A: Around 600.

    B: Yikes!

    10.Asking…

    A: Look at this sweater.

    B: Nice. Is it wool?

    A: 100%. It’s beautiful.

    B: What are they asking?

    A: Only 300 dollars!

    B: Yikes!
