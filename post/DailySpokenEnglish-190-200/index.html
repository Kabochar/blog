DailySpokenEnglish 190 200

2020年5月7日
| 阅读

第 20 天…

1.suit yourself ~ Choose something you like. 你自便

A: Bacon and eggs again?

B: It’s my favorite breakfast.

A: Whoa! Too much pepper!

B: No!I like my eggs black!

A: Suit yourself~

2.To blend in. TO take something mix whih something.

A: Do you know about thechameleon?

B: Sure, it can blend in with itsenvironment to hide.

A: Right, but you’ re wrong! Itscolor changes NOT toprotect itself.

B: Really? Then why?

A: Its color changes accordingto its mood!

3.To stick out like a sore thumb.

A: We studied “blend in” yesterday in 191h

B: Wow, your Korean is really good.

A: Do you like living in Korea?

B: Sure!

A: Do you blend in?

B: No way! I’m a white guy! I stick out like a sore thumb!

4.Stupid is as stupid does.

A: What are you doing?

B: Changing a light bulb.

A: Stupid is as stupid does.

B: Hey!I have a higher IQthan you-~~whoa!!

5.Go to school VS go to the school.

A: I’m going to the school.

B: You go to school there, too?

A: No.I’m going the school.

B: I’m meeting a friend.

A: Oh.I’ ll go with.I’ ve gota class.

6.Here you go! There you go.

A: Here you go.

B: Thank you, waiter. Hey, honey, you want some?

A: SureAAThere you goAA

B: Thanks, sweetie!

7.How do you like them apples!!

A: 20 coins? No way!

B: Wanna bet?

A: 5 bucks!

B: Great… How do you likethem apples?!

8.Recollect..remember..recal..memorize..memory…

A: What’s your favoritechildhood memory?

B: To the best of my recollection..…

A: going to Disneyland!

B: I remember going to first grade.

A: It was so great~~I hated school. Too many thingsto memorize.

B: Anyway… sweet memoriesAA

9.We might as well do.. I should do thing, don’t care..

A: What should we eat tonight?

B: I don’t know..…

A: Hmm… We might as welleat hamburgers.

B: Yeah. Might as well. We’ re Americans..…

10.My – is not up to task.

A: Jacques,I want you totranslate this into French.

B: What? But my French isnot up to task.

A: What?! You’ re French.

B: Oh,I mean, my Englishis not up to task!

11.To put somebody on.

A: I might put somebody onat the office.

B: What about my brother John?

A: Are you putting me on?

B: What? He’s smart.

A: Maybe, but he’s gotten firedfrom every job he’s ever had!
