第 33 天…

1.To knock yourself out. 不要麻烦自己

A: I’ ll give you a ride to the airport tomorrow.

B: No! Don’t knock yourself out～

A: It’s no problem. I’m going near there anyway.

B: Oh! Wonderful, then^^

2.To tough it out. Don’t give up.

A: It’s so cold. Turn on the gas.

B: No. Gas is expensive.

A: But this wood burning stoveis not enough.

B: Tough it out! Be a man!

A: But..…I’m a woman~~Same thing! Be a WoMAN!

3.To butt out. Get out of here.

A: What are you guys talking about?

B: Butt out.

A :Come on. Tell me～～

B: Butt out! This isn’t about you!

4.To belt out a song. To sing a song powerfully.

A: Hey,you can really belt out a tune!

B: Come on~Every one can sing Jingle Bells!

A: Yeah,but you were really jazzy!

B: Well,thanks^^

5.The holidays season(sometimes Holiday Season). 假期季节，有时是节日季节

A: Are you doing anything this holiday season?

B: Visiting grandma!You?

A: Same with us!

B: Have a happy holidays!

6.Bah! Humbug! 呸，骗子

A: Are you looking forward to Christmas?

B: Bah! Humbug!

A: Oh, come on! Santa won’t like that～

B: Bah! Humbug! There is not Santa.

7.Holiday cheer. 节日快乐

A: Shane, belt out some holiday cheer!

B: I’m not in the mood.

A: Come on, Scrooge!

B: Bah! Humbug!

8.R.I.P (Rest In Peace.). When someone dead, say it to show repeat.

A: Did you hear the news?

B: What?

A: Nelson Mandela died.

B: Aw, what a shame. He did great things. May he rest in peace.

9.A stickler. 一个黏人

A: How fast are you going?

B: Uh…103 km/hr exactly.

A: Better slow down to a hundred.

B: Man. You’ re such a stickler.

10.Zip it. Close your mouth.

A: I think your idea is terrible.

B: Be quiet.

A: It’s all wrong.

B: Zip it!

A: You don’t know..…

B: QUIET!
