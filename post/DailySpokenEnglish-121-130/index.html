第 13 天…

1.Another nail in the coffin. /The final nail in the coffin. 形容商业的失败举动。

A: The iPhone 4 power cabledoesn’t work on the iPhone 5.

B: That’s stupid.

A: This is another nailin their coffin.

B: They’ re not doing wellsince Steve Jobs died..…

2.high-profile.

A: We need to grow our classes.

B: Yes.We need to be morehigh-profile.

A: Any ideas on how wedo that?

B: Buy everyone who joinsa free car?

3.It spoke to me. The song spoke to me. Means: They taking to me.

A: This painting is SO ugly. Whydid you buy it?

B: It spoke to me.

A: What did it say?

B: No! Look at it. It showsthe meaing of life..…

4.Grow up! Means: to be a adult. Don’t be a children.

A: Are you playing a video game?

B: No. It’s a simulation game.

A: Grow up!

B: Look at you! Wearing"Hello Kitty"pajamas.

5.He’s mature for his age. 超越年龄（行为，智商）

A: Is your daughter readingShakespeare?

B: Yeah! She loves that stuff!

A: Isn’t she like…8 years old?

B: Yeah! She’s mature for　her age!

6.the black sheep. Means: 不幸的

A: I was such a black sheepwhen I was young.

B: People hated you?

A: Not really, they just didn’ tunderstand me.

B: You seem fine now.

A: Yeah!I think I was justmature for mv age!

7.I’m going to…

A: I’m going to go to the store.

B: I’m going to go to the store, too.

A: I’m going to buy beer.

B: I’m going to buy chocolate.

8.(to be)few and far between. Really good!

A: The food here is really good．

B: Nice restaurants like this arefew and far between.

A: I agree. How’d you find it?

B: Online!

9.Man it up!

A: Dad,I’m tired. Can we rest?

B: Only a little more to the top, son!

A: My legs hurt, dad.

B: Man it up, son! Man it up!

10.Intense. 怒目而视

A: Put some sunscreen on.

B: I’ ll only be outside for 20 minutes.

A: Those UV rays are intense.

B: Okay~Where is it?
