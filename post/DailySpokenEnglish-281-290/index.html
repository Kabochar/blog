DailySpokenEnglish 281 290

2020年5月7日
| 阅读

第 29 天…

1.Give a shout out to ~

A: Welcome to the podcast, Country Shane.

B: I love LME! Lemme give a shout out to my mom!

A: Respect your mother～

B: I love you, mom!

2.To get cold feet. When we nervous.

A: How was your date last night?

B: Oh..I didn’t go.

A: She canceled?

B: Actually…I didn’t even call.

A: You got cold feet!

3.Save it for a rainy day. 存起来，以备不时之需

A: Where are you going?

B: Shopping!

A: You don’t need anything～Hello? It’s fall–I need shoes.

B: Save your money for a rainy day.

A: Oh! Rain boots~Thanks!

4.Trendy. Popular.

A: I hate those guys.

B: Why?

A: They’ re nice. They’ re cool.

B: But they try to be so trendy.

A: Trendy’s not bad. You don’t have to hate.

B: I hate trends. I wanna be me.

5.Stay ahead of the game. To have advantage.

A: You are such a smart investor.

B: Well,I try to stay ahead of the game.

B: But how? Do you know powerful people?

A: No!I watch business trends closely.

6.Pretty beat ~ Tired.

A: Let’s play another game.

B: I’m pretty beat.

A: You’ re tired from playing tic-tac-toe?

B: It takes a lot of concentration!

7.A crock. 这是假的

A: This site is a crock.

B: What’s wrong?

A: The lessons are boring, expensive and ZERO interaction.

B: You should join DDM.

8.it’s not carved in stone.

A: How often are Shane’s podcasts?

B: Every ten days.

A: So.… tomorrow’s a new one!

B: Well, it’s not carved in stone～

9.One too many. You done the thing to much.

A: Are you angry?

B: Do you like Carol?

A: No! She’s just a friend.

B: You smiled at her one time too many.

10.Cut me some slack ~ 让我松懈下

A: I want you home by 10.

B: Alright, dad.

A: nd don’t spend more than 20 bucks.

B: Cut me some slack, dad.

A: I’ll be good!
