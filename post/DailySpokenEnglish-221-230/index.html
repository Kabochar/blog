DailySpokenEnglish 221 230

2020年5月7日
| 阅读

第 23 天…

1.Great taste. High quality.

A: He fed you Doritos on your birthday?

B: Yes. Original flavor.

A: Oh… Great taste.

B: Hello! Those are the best.

2.What’s all the racket? What is all they going on there?

A: What’s all the racket?

B: Construction.

A: Again?

B: They’ re tearing down the building next door.

3.Pipe down. Shut down. Be quiet. Keep the volume down

A: You’d better pipe down.

B: Why?

B: Dad just got home. He’s got a headache.

A: Oh. Thanks for telling me.

4.devastated. 满目疮痍. Very sad.

A: Can you order these chocolates for me?

B: Sorry, baby. No international orders.

A: Are you okay?

B: I’m devastated.

A: They look so good-~Don’t worry, sugar. I’ll find some better chocolates.

5.To give the green light.

A: What are you doing?

B: I’m planning my vacation to lceland.

A: The boss said you can take the time off?

B: He gave me the greenlight 5 minutes ago!

6.Dirt poor. 邋遢的穷人.

A: Hey, John. You wanna go to lceland with me?

B: What?I’m dirt poor.

A: Come on ~ I’lI loan you the money.

B: And charge me 20% interest? No thanks.

7.What‘s all the fuss about? 为什么大惊小怪？

A: What’s all the fuss about?

B: Mom doesn’t want to have a birthday party.

A: You told her? You shouldn’t have.

B: You know I can’t keep a secret~~

8.Down and out. 潦倒. Depressed.

A: You look down and out, Shane.

B: We lost the game.

A: You still have another chance, don’t you?

B: Yes, one more chance tomorrow.

A: Great! So you’ re down but not out^^

9.To go Dutch.

Means: I only pay what I order(In the resturant). A A 制.

A: I really had a really great dinner.

B: Your steak looked wonderful.

A: And so did your hotdog.

B: AIright…do you mind if we go Dutch?

10.Get dressed up. Good dressed.

A: What are you getting all dressed up for?

B: Tonight’s Beiber’s concert. Baby, baby, baby~Oh~~

A: Justin gonna sing that song to you?

B: Yes! I’ ll always be his.
