第 56 / 70 篇…

1. To chime in. 插话

A: I completely disagreed with John.

B: Really?

A: Yeah. He was totally wrong.

B: Why didn’t you chime in?

2. Long-winded. 冗长

A: Are you watching C-Span?

B: Yeah.

A: Those guys are so long-winded. How can you watch?

B: It helps me fall asleep.

3. To get into a groove. 一成不变

A: Did you ride this morning?

B: No,I got up late.

A: Come on! You need to exercise.

B: I know. I need to get into a groove.

4. Lemming，sheep，sheeple. 多动脑，不要只会尾随

A: Dude, your phone is so old.

B: It still works.

A: And when’s the last time you bought clothes?

B: I don’t need any.

A: Come on!!

B: Hey,I’m not a lemming! I do things my own way.

5. To eyeball something. 要注意一些事情

A: How many boxes are you gonna need?

B: I have no idea.

A: Just eyeball your stuff and guess.

B: Um…12 maybe?

6. In a huff. Angry.

A: Where’s Shane going in a huff?

B: He’s mad again.

A: What’d you do?

B: I told him his jokes weren’t funny.

7. A knee-jerk reaction / response. 下意识反应

A: What do you think of the riots?

B: Well, my knee-jerk reaction is to support the people.

A: But?

B: But, after I thought about it, violence with violence never solves anything.

8. Drained. 疲惫

A: You look tired.

B: I’m totally drained.

A: What happened?

B: Eh~Some people expect too much.

9. Buff.

A: You’ re pretty buff.

B: Yeah?I’ ve been working out a bit.

A: Great. Any reason?

B: Not really. I just like the exercise.

10. Yes，siree，Bob！

A: I’d like to upgrade my internet speed to Ultra.

B: No problem.

A: Can you come out with the new modem tomorrow?

B: Yes, siree, Bob!
