DailySpokenEnglish 201 210

2020年5月7日
| 阅读

第 21 天…

1.trip VS travel VS journey.

A: Do you like to travel?

B: Not really.

A: I prefer short trips.

B: So, you’ ve never been overseas?

A: Never. I haven’t even seen the ocean!

2.jumping on the wrong bandwagon.

A: Semi-Finals T-shirts for the 2014 World Cup. 50% off!

B: Who’s on it?

A: Team USA! We’ re going to the Semi-Finals!

B: The USA?! Ha! You’ re jumping on the wrong bandwagon!

3.I just don’t happen to.. Yes, but not this./ Yes, but not this moment.

A: It’s Sunday. You’ re at home.

B: Yeah?

A: I thought you were religious.

B: I am. I just don’t happen to go to church.

4.willpower. 意志

A: You quit smoking!

B: Yeah! Two months now.

A: Wow! That’s great.

B: How’d you do it?

A: Willpower.100% willpower.

5.A trumps B. A defeat B.

A: I need to study. Be quiet!

B: The living room is nota good place to study.

A: Says who?

B: Environment trumps willpower. Go to the library.

6.I’m looking into ~ing. I’m looking inter…

A: What are you searching for?

B: I’m looking into buying a juicer.

A: Good for you. Tryingto be healthier?

B: I’m trying^^

7.frazzled. tired.

A: You look frazzled.

B: It’s the city life. I feelboxed in.

A: What are you gonna do?

B: I’m looking into moving to the country.

8.I hava a hankering for… Really want to do something.

A: What do you want for dinner?

B: Well , I have a hankering for donuts.

A: Donuts? They’ re for breakfast.

B: So!I want jelly-filled donuts!

9.Not nearly enough.

A: I love you, honey.

B: Really? Then give mea foot rub.

A: Gross.I don’t like feet.

B: You don’t love menearly enough.

A: Shoulders?.

B: No! My aching, stinking feet. NOW!

10.I even (+verb). I enev do something.

A: You really love to cook, don’t you?

B: Yes, I even love to washdishes!

A: You’ re crazy.

B: You’ re lazy!
