DailySpokenEnglish 301 310

2020年5月7日

第 31 天…

1.To pick someone’s brain. To get somebody opinion.

A: Any ideas on how to improve business?

B: No. We need someone to help us.

A: Why don’t we pick Jason’s brain?

B: Jason? From IBM?

A: Great idea^^

2.A train wreck. Something is bad.

A: Justin Biber was in trouble again.

B: They guy’s a train wreck.

A: That’s what fame does.

B: No. That’s what no discipline does.

3.Pushing up daisies.

A: John’s drinking and smoking again~

B: Yeah. He had a bad week.

A: If he keeps that up, he’ll be pushing up daisies.

B: How can we get him to stop?

4.Out in the sticks. Far in the country.

A: I heard Shane moved.

B: Yeah. He’s out in the sticks now.

A: How far out?

B: Far. He lives on a mountain with no neighbors!

5.Spring ahead, fall back.

A: You still didn’t change this clock?

B: Woops~Wait..… is it 5 or 7?

A: Spring forward, fall back!

B: Thank you! That gives me another hour to prepare dinner-～

6.How does that strike you? How do that shock you?

A: How did Obama’s recent comment strike you?

B: Which one?

A: When he said “I’m really good at killing people.”

B: What? He never said that. That’d be crazy.

A: Look it up.

7.A little too strange. 有点太奇怪了

A: Do you like Lady Gaga’ s new song?

B: I don’t think I’ve ever heard it.

A: But it’s everywhere She’s the greatest~

B: Eh~She’s a little too strange for me.

8.It’s raining cats and dogs. 倾盆大雨

A: Hey, mom! How are you?

B: Who is this?

A: Your son–Shane.

B: Oh,I’m fine.

A: That’s good! Why is it so loud there?

B: It’s raining cats and dogs!

9.Back in the saddle. Everything is continue.

A: Hey, long time no see!

B: Yeah,I was super busy.

A: It’s good to see you exercising again^^

B: Yes!I’m back in the saddle!

10.All too much. To many.

A: You shouldn’t eat that.

B: It’s not healthy.

A: Okay..…

B: Don’t use margarine, use butter.

A: Okay. This health stuff is all to much for me.
