    第 50 / 70 篇…

    1. Gibberish. None sense.

    A: So, how is Shane’s DDM class?

    B: Well, the news stories are pretty easy, but that situation comedy- Uah!A bunch of gibberish’

    A: Well, situation comedies give you real, daily English. Study hard!

    B: I know~I won’t give up!

    2. To be tied up with something. To be busy with something.

    A: You wanna go watch a movie?

    B: I’d like to, but I can’t.

    A: Maybe tomorrow?

    B: I don’t think I’ll be able to.I’m really tied up with work these days.

    3. Catch a movie/game. 追，捕捉

    A: Yo, Pete, what’s up this weekend?

    B: Not much.

    A: You wanna go catch a game?

    B: Sounds great. How about tomorrow?

    4. To catch up. 赶上来

    A: What else we need?

    B: Ketchup. You go down that aisle. I’ II catch up with you in a minute.

    A: But I don’t know what kind of ketchup to buy.

    B: Organic! Duh~~

    5. Duh~~! Yes! When somebody ask a stupid question.

    A: So, you have an online business?

    B: Yes,I teach online.

    A: Oh. So, you use a computer?

    B: Duh~～!

    6. To be on thin ice. In a danger situation. 如履薄冰

    A: Where are you?

    B: I’m still at work.

    A: It’s 9 PM!

    B: I came in late two days in a row. I’m on thin ice here～～

    7. Some T-shirt，huh? Terrible meaning.

    A: Oh~I’m full!

    B: Yeah, me, too. Some dinner, huh?

    A: When it comes to cooking, mom’s is the best.

    B: Yes! Thanks, mom^^

    8. Likeminded. 志同道合

    A: You guys make great business partners.

    B: You wanna know the secret?

    A: Yeah!

    B: We’ re likeminded. That’s so important.

    9. I need to get out more. I need to get a lot.

    A: What’s the last movie you saw?

    B: In a theater? Um… oh! The Titanic. Great movie.

    B: What? That was over 15 years ago!

    A: Yeah…I need to get out more~

    10. coming along. 来吧

    A: How’s the homework coming along?

    B: It’s almost done.

    A: It has to be done before you meet your friends.

    B: Don’t worry. It’ ll be done.
