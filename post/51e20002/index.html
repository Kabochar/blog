Daily Spoken English 11~20

2020年5月7日
| 英语
文章目录

第 2 天更新…

1. I have a long week.

A: You look stressed.

B: Well，I have a long week.

A: Already? What happened?

B: Just work. I have a new project this’s taking forever.

2. Thank god, it’s Friday! TGIF!

A: Ugh! Thank god, it’s Friday.

B: No kidding! Are you going out tonight?

A: No way! I’ve got FNL~~

B: Hava fun~~

3. That’s pure nonsense.

A: You’re sitting to close to the TV.

B: I’m comfortable.

A: Your eyesight is going to get worse.

B: That’s pure nonsense.

4. Did you get out shopping?

A: Did you get out hunting this weekend?

B: Yesterday morning.

A: Did you get anything?

B: Nothing but really cold feet!

5. (to) do away with something

A: I need to do away with my knuckle cracking.

B: It’s a hard habit to break.

A: I know. It’s almost impossible.

B: Want me to help?

6. (to) do away with something

A: Are you gonna do it up for your mom’s birthday?

B: No，we’re just gonna have a nice dinner at home.

A: No party?

B: She said she didn’t want one.

7. (to) do up something(coat, tie, dress)

A: Is it cold outside，mom?

B: Yes. Be sure to do up your jacket.

A: Okay. Where’s my hat?

B: It’s in your pocket.

8. to do it over.

A: How do you like my picture, daddy?

B: What is it?

A: It’s a picture of a bear.

B: Oh… um… you should do it over.

9. Because of…

A: Why didn’t you call me last night?

B: Because of my phone’s battery.

A: Why didn’t you charge it?

B: Because of my carelessness. I left my cord at school.

10. Thanks to…

A: Your skin looks healthy!

B: It’s all thanks to this lotion.

A: You mean，thanks to me for buying you the lotion.

B: Right!
