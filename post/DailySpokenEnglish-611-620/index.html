Daily Spoken English 611~611

2020年5月7日
| 英语
文章目录

第 62 / 70 篇…

1. Wrapped around someone’s finger. 任意摆布某人

A: What are you doing? Grocery shopping?

B: Yeah～

A: She’s got you wrapped around her finger!

B: I do it because I love her.

2. To feel a little warm. I started to feel sick.

A: Ugh..…

B: You don’t look good.

A: I feel a little warm.

B: Let’s take your temperature.

3. Nauseous. 恶心

A: You okay?

B: I’m feeling a bit nauseous.

A: Have you thrown up?

B: No，but I think I might.

4. The runs. 赛跑

A: I don’t think I can go in today.

B: What’s wrong?

A: My stomach. I’ve got the runs.

B: I’ll call your boss. Turn the fan on.

5. Freeloader. 不速之客

A: Why do you let Freddie just eat your food?

B: I don’t know. He doesn’t eat much.

A: He’s a freeloader!

B: He’s my brother! Let him be～～

6. A knockoff. 一个仿冒品

A: What’s wrong with your shirt, there? What?

B: The guy and the horse– they’ re in color.

A: Yeah，so? It’s a Polo.

B: No, dude. It’s a knockoff!

7. Tupperware. 特百惠

A: More Tupperware?

B: My mom’s got tons.

A: You reuse it，right?

B: Sure! Well，I bring the dirty ones back to her～

8. To sponge off. 依赖某人；擦掉

A: More Tupperware?

B: Yeah! My mom made spaghetti.

A: When are you going to quit sponging off your mom? You’ re 46!

B: She was just going to throw it away~~~

9. An old-timer. 老朋友

A: When did you start teaching on YouTube?

B: Back in 2010.

A: Oh，so you’ re an old-timer.

B: Not really,a few go back to 2006.

10. A show of hands. 举手表示

A: With a show of hands–how many of you like English?Excellent!How many of you know Coach Shane? Wow!! Everyone?

B: Of course! YOU are Coach Shane!Duh~~~
